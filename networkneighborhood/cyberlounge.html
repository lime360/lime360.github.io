CyberLounge | Discord Server

The Network Neighborhood likes to stay in touch with the community, as well as members of the 'ring. Feel free to stop by and have a chat! You could discuss web development, video gaming, technology, pogs, whatever you feel like.

Discord Connection Info

Go to the CyberLounge server's Instant Invite page to assign yourself a nickname. Once you're signed in, it's a good idea to claim your account to ensure that nobody can impersonate you. Once that's complete, you can choose to either continue using the HTML-based web chat or download the Discord Client for PC, Mac & Mobile. Linux support is coming soon.

A Note to IRC Users

The CyberLounge IRC channel is being phased out due to inactivity. We chose to move to Discord because of its extensive feature set, dedicated development team, friendly user interface and cross-platform capabilities. If you still use this channel, please be aware that Network Neighborhood members will no longer be available there and conversation will be unmoderated. Migrating to the new Discord server is highly recommended.
